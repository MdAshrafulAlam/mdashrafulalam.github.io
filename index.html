Short Biography

I am a Research Associate of School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University. I am affiliated with the Computer Networks and Communications Lab (CNCL) advised by Prof. Jun Zhao. Here, I am involved in research works on AI and Blockchain applications to the Energy and Vehicular Networks. My research interests are mainly focused on advanced communication, security and resource allocation techniques for future wireless networks. I am passionate about learning, teaching and research. Moreover, I am serving as a reviewer for several major IEEE journals, magazines and conferences. I received the MS in Computer Science and the BS in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Jahangirnagar University and International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh, respectively. I am a member of IEEE, IEEE ComSoc and IEEE VT. My personal webpage is: https://mbaqer.github.io.
